Welcome to cricket rules

  1. Cricket is played between two teams each made up of eleven players.
  2. Games comprise of at least one innings where each team will take turns in batting and fielding/bowling.
  3. The fielding team will have a bowler bowl the ball to the batsman who tries to hit the ball with their bat.
  4. The fielding team tries to get the batsmen out by…
  5. If the bowler bowls the ball from the wrong place then there will be no ball
  6. A “Wide Ball” will be declared if the umpire thinks the batsman did not have a reasonable opportunity to score off the delivery.
  7. A “Bye” is where a ball that isn’t a no ball or wide passes the striking batsman and runs are scored without the batsman hitting the ball.
  8. A “Leg Bye” is where runs are scored by hitting the batsman, but not the bat and the ball is not a no ball or wide.
  9. If the ball is bowled and it hits the batsman first without the bat hitting it then an LBW decision is possible.
